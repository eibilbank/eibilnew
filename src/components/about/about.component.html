About Eibil Nidhi Limited

Eibil Nidhi Limited is a mutual benefit company, registered under the Companies Act 2013. Our primary objective is to cultivate the habit of thrift and savings amongst our members, receiving deposits from, and lending to, our members only, for their mutual benefit.

We are built on a foundation of trust, transparency, and integrity. Our team is dedicated to providing personalized financial services and support to help our members achieve their financial aspirations. We believe in growing together as a strong, financially-empowered community.
